Proposed Solution (Core Idea)
AI-powered real-time voice translation system
Translates word-by-word as speech is uttered, not after completion
Enables users to: 1. Speak naturally in their own language 2. Be understood instantly in another
language
Designed to work in: 1. Public infrastructure 2. Transport 3. Offices 4. Tourism and healthcare
Built for mass accessibility, not niche users
